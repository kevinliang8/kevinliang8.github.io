I am a third-year PhD student in Linguistics at UCLA.

Broadly speaking, my research interests lie in the areas of computational modelling, language acquisition, and psycholinguistics. I have also done work in phonetics and theoretical phonology.

Outside of research, I serve as the co-chair for the organizing comittee of NACLO, and I have written problems for NACLO, UKLO, and OzCLO.
